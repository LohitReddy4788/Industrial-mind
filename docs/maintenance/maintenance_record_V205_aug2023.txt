INSPECTION SCOPE:
Statutory external and internal inspection as required by Factories Act Section 31 and
OISD-118 Section 12. Previous inspection: Aug-2021. Due date per schedule: Aug-2023.

VESSEL DATA:
- Design Pressure: 8.5 barg
- Operating Pressure: 5.2-5.8 barg
- Design Temperature: 120°C
- Operating Temperature: 68-75°C
- Material: Carbon Steel SA-516 Gr 70
- Year of Manufacture: 2009 (14 years old)
- Corrosion Allowance: 3.2mm (original)

INSPECTION FINDINGS:

External Inspection:
- Surface corrosion (paint failure): observed on 3 locations — bottom skirt and 2 nozzle areas
- Insulation condition: 15% moisture ingress detected by thermography
- Support condition: base plate corrosion, 0.8mm estimated depth — acceptable

Internal Inspection (vessel decontaminated and gas-freed per SOP-012):
1. Shell thickness measurements — 32 points surveyed using UT (Olympus 38DL Plus):
   - Minimum recorded: 14.8mm (bottom quadrant, 2.1m from east end)
   - Required minimum (per API-510): 14.2mm (based on corrosion allowance consumed)
   - Remaining corrosion allowance: 0.6mm
   - Corrosion rate: 0.16 mm/year (last 2 years)
   - Calculated remaining life: 3.75 years (next inspection MANDATORY by Aug-2025)

2. Weld inspection (20% of welds by MT):
   - 3 locations showed minor surface indications — judged acceptable per ASME VIII Div.1
   - No through-wall defects detected

3. Nozzle inspection:
   - Inlet nozzle (N1-DN150): 2mm buildup of deposits — cleaned
   - Safety valve nozzle (N4-DN50): free and clear
   - IMPORTANT: Drain nozzle (N6-DN25) shows weld deterioration at nozzle-shell junction.
     Ultrasonics confirm 1.2mm thinning at weld toe. RECOMMEND: repair welding at next opportunity.
     Currently safe to operate but must be re-inspected within 12 months.

4. Internal surface: general corrosion, no pitting exceeding 15% depth allowance

OVERPRESSURE PROTECTION:
- PSV-V205A: tested and found to set at 9.1 barg (design: 9.5 barg). Within acceptable range.
- PSV-V205B (standby): not tested this cycle — due for test within 6 months per OISD-118 Section 12.4

OUTCOME:
Vessel APPROVED FOR CONTINUED SERVICE subject to:
1. Drain nozzle repair (N6): complete by Dec-2023
2. PSV-V205B test: complete by Feb-2024
3. Next full inspection: Aug-2025 (mandatory — remaining life margin)
4. Repair insulation moisture ingress: Priority Medium (schedule in next shutdown)
