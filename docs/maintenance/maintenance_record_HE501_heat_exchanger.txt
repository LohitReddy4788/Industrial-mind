INSPECTION EVENT — January 2024
Work Order: WO-2024-0034
Type: Statutory Inspection (3-year interval as per Factories Act Rule 61)
Inspection Authority: Chief Inspector of Factories, Maharashtra
Inspector: Shri. A.K. Patil (CIF No. MH-2847)

Pre-Inspection Operating Conditions Review:
- U overall (measured): 312 W/m²K vs design 385 W/m²K (19% reduction — fouling)
- Shell side pressure drop: 1.8 bar (design: 0.9 bar) — 2x increase (fouling/blockage)
- Crude preheat duty loss: Estimated 8.5 MMkcal/hr shortfall
- Additional furnace fuel consumption: ₹4.2 lakhs/month due to fouling

Inspection Findings:
TUBE SIDE:
- 23 tubes found partially plugged (salt deposits + crude wax)
- 8 tubes failed eddy current test (>30% wall loss) — REQUIRES PLUGGING
- Tubesheet face corrosion: Moderate (acceptable, <2mm pitting)
- Tube-to-tubesheet joint: 3 joints showed minor weeping (hydrostatic test)

SHELL SIDE:
- Heavy coke/asphaltene fouling on shell side baffles (30-40mm thick deposit)
- Baffle spacing erosion: 2 baffles found loose (vibration erosion)
- Shell nozzle N3 (residue outlet) showed 3.2mm wall thinning (UT measurement)
  Minimum required wall: 6.0mm, Measured: 7.1mm — ACCEPTABLE but monitor
- No stress corrosion cracking found (WFMT inspection)

Statutory Fitness Certificate Status: CONDITIONAL PASS
Conditions:
1. Plug 8 failed tubes immediately before restart
2. Shell nozzle N3: Increase UT monitoring frequency to 6-monthly
3. Chemical cleaning mandatory before restart
4. Next inspection due: January 2027

Actions Taken:
1. High-pressure water jet cleaning — tube side (350 bar, 180°C hot water)
2. Chemical cleaning — shell side (DDBSA acid descaling, neutralization, passivation)
3. Plugged 8 failed tubes with SS tapered plugs (2.7% of tubes — within 10% acceptable limit)
4. Re-rolled 3 weeping tube-to-tubesheet joints
5. Replaced 2 loose baffles
6. Pressure tested: Tube side 27 bar, Shell side 18 bar (1.5x design) — NO LEAKS

Post-Cleaning Performance:
- U overall: 368 W/m²K (95.6% of design — excellent recovery)
- Shell pressure drop: 1.0 bar (back to near design)
- Crude outlet temperature recovered to 212°C

Costs:
- Cleaning (HP water + chemical): ₹8,40,000
- Tube plugging and rolling: ₹1,20,000
- Baffle replacement: ₹85,000
- Inspection fees: ₹45,000
- Total: ₹10,90,000

Recommendation: Implement online fouling monitoring (inlet/outlet temperature and dP trending) to schedule next cleaning at optimal point before 15% U-degradation.
