MAINTENANCE WORK ORDER RECORD
=============================
Work Order No: WO-2023-0614
Date: 12-Jun-2023
Equipment Tag: P-101
Equipment Name: Centrifugal Pump — Crude Desalter Feed
Location: Unit-3, Refinery Block B
Technician: Rajesh Kumar (Senior Maintenance Technician)
Supervisor: Amit Shah (Maintenance Engineer)

REASON FOR MAINTENANCE:
Vibration readings on P-101 exceeded alarm threshold of 7.5 mm/s RMS. DCS log shows
vibration trending up from 4.2 mm/s (normal) to 9.1 mm/s over 14 days.
Operator Suresh Nair reported unusual grinding noise from bearing housing on 10-Jun-2023.

OBSERVATIONS ON INSPECTION:
- Mechanical seal showed severe erosion on the stationary seat. Scoring visible on seal face.
- Seal leakage rate estimated at 8-10 drops per minute (allowable: max 3 drops/min per OISD-118 Section 6.2)
- Inboard bearing (DE) showed pitting corrosion — bearing temperature was 72°C vs normal 45-50°C
- Impeller: minor cavitation marks on suction side, estimated 8% degradation — acceptable
- Coupling alignment: 0.15mm angular misalignment detected — corrected to 0.02mm
- Mechanical seal failure mode: seal failure due to dry running incident on 04-Jun-2023 (recorded in DCS)

ROOT CAUSE:
Primary cause — Mechanical seal dry running on 04-Jun-2023 when suction valve closed
inadvertently during shift changeover. Duration of dry run: approximately 3-4 minutes.
No operator alarm was triggered because the seal dry-run detector (SDD-P101) was not armed.

ACTIONS TAKEN:
1. Replaced mechanical seal — Type: John Crane Type 1B, 65mm bore (Part No: JC-1B-065-SIC/CARBON)
2. Replaced inboard bearing (DE) — SKF 6313-2RS (Lot No: SKF2023-44771)
3. Flushed seal flush system — checked and cleaned Plan 11 flush line orifice (found 40% blocked)
4. Corrected coupling alignment to 0.02mm
5. Raised maintenance notification for SDD-P101 inspection (Notif No: MN-2023-0891)
6. Flow control valve FCV-12A also cleaned — found 15% fouling on plug

MEASUREMENTS AFTER REPAIR:
- Vibration: 3.8 mm/s RMS (DE), 2.9 mm/s RMS (NDE) — within acceptable limits
- Bearing temperature: 47°C (DE), 44°C (NDE)
- Seal leakage: 0 drops/minute
- Flow: 142 m³/hr (design: 145 m³/hr) — acceptable

PARTS USED:
- Mechanical seal John Crane 1B: 1 unit
- Bearing SKF 6313-2RS: 1 unit (DE bearing)
- Seal flush orifice 3mm: 1 unit
- Gaskets and O-rings: 1 set

NEXT MAINTENANCE DUE:
- Seal inspection: 6 months (Dec-2023)
- Bearing vibration check: monthly
- Coupling alignment check: annual PM due Jun-2024
- SDD-P101 test: before next startup — mandatory per OISD-118

Regulatory Reference: OISD-118 Section 6.2 (Rotating Equipment Maintenance), Factory Act Section 31 (Safety of Machinery)
