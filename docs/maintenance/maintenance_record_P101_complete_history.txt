MAINTENANCE EVENT 1 — February 2022
Date: 14-Feb-2022
Work Order: WO-2022-0089
Type: Corrective Maintenance
Reported By: Operator Ramesh Kumar (Shift A)
Reported Fault: High vibration alarm at 12.5 mm/s RMS (Trip setpoint: 15 mm/s). Unusual noise from DE bearing.

Root Cause Analysis:
- Found grease hardening and contamination in drive-end (DE) bearing (SKF 6316)
- Bearing inner race showed pitting corrosion — classic sign of false brinelling from standby vibration
- Seal flush line (API Plan 53B) had partial blockage reducing barrier fluid flow by 40%
- Vibration spectrum showed 1X and 2X peaks indicating rotor imbalance contributed

Actions Taken:
1. Isolated pump, applied LOTO (Lockout-Tagout) as per PTW-2022-0089
2. Drained pump casing and flushed with diesel
3. Replaced DE bearing (SKF 6316) and NDE bearing (SKF 6313)
4. Cleaned and flushed API Plan 53B seal support system
5. Replaced barrier fluid (Shell Tellus S2 V 46)
6. Balanced impeller dynamically — found 8.5g imbalance corrected to 0.3g
7. Realigned pump-motor coupling (final alignment: 0.02mm parallel, 0.01mm angular)
8. Replaced mechanical seal (Flowserve Type 28AT)

Post-Maintenance Test:
- Vibration: 2.1 mm/s RMS (Excellent — within ISO 10816-3 Zone A)
- Flow: 448 m³/hr (within 0.5% of rated)
- Current draw: 182A (rated 195A)
- No seal leakage observed

Parts Used:
- SKF 6316 Bearing x1: ₹8,400
- SKF 6313 Bearing x1: ₹5,200
- Flowserve 28AT Mechanical Seal x1: ₹1,24,000
- Barrier Fluid 5L x2: ₹3,600
- Gaskets and seals set: ₹2,800
Total Cost: ₹1,44,000

MAINTENANCE EVENT 2 — June 2023
Date: 22-Jun-2023
Work Order: WO-2023-0341
Type: Planned Preventive Maintenance (Annual Overhaul)
Performed By: Mechanical Team + OEM Representative (Kirloskar)

Inspection Findings:
- Impeller wear ring clearance: 0.85mm (Acceptable limit: <1.0mm; OEM new clearance: 0.25mm)
- Casing wear ring clearance: 0.95mm (approaching limit)
- Shaft deflection: 0.08mm TIR (Acceptable: <0.05mm) — EXCEEDED LIMIT
- DE Bearing temperature trending up: 68°C average (Alert: 75°C, Trip: 85°C)
- Mechanical seal: No leakage, face wear within limits
- Impeller: Minor cavitation pitting on suction eye (estimated 3-5% efficiency loss)

Actions Taken:
1. Replaced shaft (new shaft from OEM — SS410 material)
2. Replaced impeller wear rings (Bronze — reduces galling risk)
3. Replaced casing wear rings
4. Replaced DE and NDE bearings (preventive)
5. Impeller cleaned — pitting polished but not replaced (cost-benefit decision, supervisor approval)
6. Full alignment check — corrected to 0.015mm parallel, 0.008mm angular
7. Coupling elastomer replaced (Lovejoy L110 spider)
8. All fasteners torqued to specification
