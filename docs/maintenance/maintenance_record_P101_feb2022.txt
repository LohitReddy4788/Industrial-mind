MAINTENANCE WORK ORDER RECORD
=============================
Work Order No: WO-2022-0218
Date: 18-Feb-2022
Equipment Tag: P-101
Equipment Name: Centrifugal Pump — Crude Desalter Feed
Location: Unit-3, Refinery Block B
Technician: Suresh Nair (Maintenance Technician)
Supervisor: Priya Menon (Reliability Engineer)

REASON FOR MAINTENANCE:
Planned preventive maintenance — 12-month interval per PM schedule.
Also: operator report of intermittent seal leakage (~2 drops/min) since 10-Feb-2022.

OBSERVATIONS ON INSPECTION:
- Mechanical seal: seal faces showed minor wear, acceptable but approaching replacement threshold
- API Plan 11 flush line: found 25% blocked by accumulated deposits — partially restricting flush flow
- Bearing condition: vibration 3.2 mm/s (DE), 2.8 mm/s (NDE) — within normal range
- Coupling: alignment within tolerance (0.03mm angular)
- Motor insulation resistance: 850 MΩ — acceptable
- NOTE: This is the 3rd seal inspection in 24 months on P-101. Pattern of rapid seal wear noted
  and flagged to Reliability team for investigation (Pattern noticed by Priya Menon).

ACTIONS TAKEN:
1. Replaced mechanical seal faces (stationary and rotary) — preventive replacement
2. Cleaned and cleared Plan 11 flush line — full flow restored
3. Checked and re-torqued all flange connections on pump casing
4. Lubricated bearings — Mobilux EP2 grease (60g per bearing)

ROOT CAUSE OF SEAL WEAR:
Investigation by Priya Menon: flush flow below minimum required (1.5 L/min).
Orifice partially blocked was allowing only 0.9 L/min. Insufficient flush
causes seal face overheating → accelerated wear. Recommend quarterly orifice check.

MEASUREMENTS AFTER REPAIR:
- Vibration: 3.1 mm/s (DE), 2.7 mm/s (NDE)
- Seal flush flow: 1.8 L/min (restored)
- No seal leakage observed

PARTS USED:
- Seal face set (stationary + rotary): 1 set
- Flush line orifice (replacement): 1 unit (precautionary)
- Grease Mobilux EP2: 120g

NEXT MAINTENANCE DUE:
- Seal inspection: Feb-2023 (12-month interval)
- Flush orifice check: May-2022 (3-month interval — increased frequency due to blocking pattern)

NOTE TO RELIABILITY: P-101 has now had seal attention in Aug-2020, Oct-2021, and Feb-2022.
Frequency is increasing. Recommend root cause analysis on seal life. Possible upgrade
to Plan 23 (seal water cooling) to extend seal life — Priya Menon to evaluate.

Regulatory Reference: OISD-118 Section 6.2
